## 病毒是什么
- [wiki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_virus)

>A computer virus is a type of malware(malicious software,恶意软件) that, when executed, replicates itself by modifying other computer programs and inserting its own code into those programs.If this replication succeeds, the affected areas are then said to be "infected" with a computer virus, a metaphor derived from biological viruses.

- 换句话说,恶意软件有很多种,病毒只是其中最出名的一类而已.
